# VeriAgent Vision

VeriAgent is a verifiable audit commitment layer for AI-agent actions.

It records structured AI-agent audit events, canonicalizes them, hashes them, and later batches them into Merkle roots that can be anchored to blockchain infrastructure.

The first goal is to prove that a record of an AI-agent action was committed at a specific point in time, has not been altered since that commitment, and is part of a verifiable execution trail.
